Browsable
The presence of the <yt:browsable> tag indicates that the corresponding category is browsable on YouTube in one or more countries. The tag's regions attribute contains a space-delimited list of two-letter regionIDs that identifies the particular countries where the category is browsable.
Deprecated
Categories that are neither assignable or browsable are deprecated and are identified as such
